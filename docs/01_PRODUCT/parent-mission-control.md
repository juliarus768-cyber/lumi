## Math Adventure Parent Mission Control Specification

Version 1.0 Parent Experience & Coaching Blueprint

## Purpose

Mission Control is the parent's private coaching hub. It is designed to provide meaningful insights, simplify reward management, and support children's learning without turning parents into supervisors.

## Design Philosophy

- Coach instead of monitor.

- Celebrate growth rather than perfection.

- Provide actionable insights.

- Reduce the need for constant reminders.

- Keep advanced controls available but unobtrusive.

## Dashboard Overview

- Weekly completion

- Pending approvals

- Learning highlights

- Quick actions

## Acceptance Criteria

- Parents understand progress within 30 seconds.

- Every recommendation is actionable.

- Reward approvals update balances correctly.

- Settings persist across sessions.

- Parent actions never interfere with adaptive learning history.
